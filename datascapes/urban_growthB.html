Urban Growth B

If the world's population reaches 10 billion by 2100 we can assume that 3 billion of these people will live in cities. This Urban Growth datascape shows the urban footprint of three billion people at four different typologies: 1) hyper density (NYC) 1, high density informal settlement (Mumbai) 2, medium density (Barcelona) 3, and suburban density (Los Angeles) 4.
